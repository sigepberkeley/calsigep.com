Balanced Man Scholarship

The entrance to Dwinelle Hall. Source: Daily Cal


The Brothers of Sigma Phi Epsilon are proud to offer the Balanced Man Scholarship.

This scholarship is awarded to incoming male freshmen of the University of California, Berkeley, who best embody the Balanced Man Ideal of Sound Mind and Sound Body.

The Ancient Greeks believed that developing a Sound Mind and a Sound Body is the way to live a fulfilling life. SigEp adopted this philosophy through the Balanced Man Ideal and since 1990 has awarded the Balanced Man Scholarship to extraordinary freshmen.

This year we will be giving out over $2,000 in scholarships.

Thank you for your interest in the Balanced Man Scholarship and we look forward to reading your application. The application is now closed. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to email or call us.
